Secondary Emotions

Secondary emotions are complex feelings that develop from combinations of primary emotions.

They are influenced by our personal experiences, social environment, and thoughts.

Understanding secondary emotions helps us manage reactions more effectively.

Remember:

1. They mix basic emotions.

2. Often influenced by culture.

3. Awareness improves emotional control.
